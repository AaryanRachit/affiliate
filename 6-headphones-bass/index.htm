6 best headphones that have powerful bass


Published on: MARCH 31, 2023

If you like heavy-bass genres like EDM or Hip-Hop, you would likely want a headphone which can add more punch to your music. While there may be lots of headphones which promise to provide heavy bass but when you actually listen to them you get near-to-no bass. After testing over 50 headphones, we have come to a conclusion.

Today, we will show 6 headphones which are good in both the quality and the price.

The 6 best headphones

Here are the 6 best headphones which you would love to listen from.

  1. Sony WH-1000XM4
  2. Sennheiser MOMENTUM 4 Wireless
  3. Skullcandy Crusher Evo Wireless
  4. Anker Soundcore Space Q45 Wireless
  5. Anker Soundcore Life Q30 Wireless
  6. Anker soundcore Life Q20 Wireless

Sony WH-1000XM4

The best headphone that I have come across with powerful bass is the Sony WH-1000XM4. I have tested this headphone myself and it really provides bass-heavy sound that delivers extra thumpy sound which is great if you listen to genres like funks or dupstep. If you are the one who prefers custom sound profile, then you can use the sound equalizer feature on this headphone to suit your tastes. Along with that, you also get high resolution audio since it has Sony's proprietary codec which most Sony headphones don't have.

If you want a headphone which has a good battery life, then you should choose this one. It has a battery life of 34 hours on a single full charge which ensures that you don't have to worry about charging it frequently. It also provides you a very comfortable fit which helps you to listen for longer periods of time. In addition to that, you get Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) to help out block any background that disturbs you and I must say that its ANC is very powerful. It has the ability to supress upto 32 decibels of the total background noise. Previously I had some problems with my headphone not connecting to multiple devices, but that's not the case with this headphone. With it's dual connection technology, it can connect to upto 2 devices at a time.

The actual price of this headphone is $385 but you can get it for just $348 on Amazon with the same quality.

Sennheiser MOMENTUM 4 Wireless

If you want a headphone which is more affordable without losing to many features, then you should definitely check out the Sennheiser MOMENTUM 4 Wireless. With this headphone, you get decent amount of bass and sound quality. This headphone also has 3-band EQ if you prefer different kind of sound profile.

With this headphone you can get upto 62 hours of continuous playback, which is 200% more battery life than the previous headphone that we have discussed. It provides you with a comfortable fit to make sure that you can wear it for long sessions. You also have Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) on this headphone which may not as much effective as Sony WH-1000XM4 but it can still supress background noises very well, which makes it a good choice at places at office where there is too much noise and disturbance. Just like the previous headphone, it supports multi-device pairing, meaning that you can connect it with up to 2 devices at a time. All-in-all, it's value for money.

If you want this headphone then you can get it on Amazon for just $287.

Skullcandy Crusher Evo Wireless

If you want a headphone at a mid-range price that can provide intense bass and meet your needs, then you can choose Skullcandy Crusher Evo Wireless. With this headphone, you get a haptic bass slider which adds extra thump or vibrations to your music. When set to high, the sound is bass boosted by a lot. It makes you feel that your skull is vibrating. This makes it great for listening to genres like EDM and Hip-Hop. The only thing that you don't get with this headphone is the Active Noise Cancellation (ANC).

This headphone can go upto 35 hours of continuous playback on a single full charge, which is good enough for long sessions. It also provides you with a comfortable fit that helps you wear it for long periods of time without getting any pain. As discussed earlier, this headphone doesn't block that much sound.

You can get this headphone for $200 on Skullcandy's official site but you can get it for just $150 on Amazon with the same quality.

Anker Soundcore Space Q45 Wireless

The Anker soundcore space Q45 Wireless is a headphone which has adaptive Noise Cancellation instead of ANC presets. This means that it can automatically adjust the noise cancellation according to your environment. This feature is very useful for me since I don't have to change the noise cancelling intensity everytime. You can switch to manual control also if you want to have more control over the noise cancellation intensity.

Moreover, you can use this headphone for upto 50 hours when ANC is on or 65 hours when the ANC is off, which can be helpful when you're travelling and there is no means of charging. It has a feature called AI-enhanced calls that identifies and picks up your voice precisely from your surroundings, no matter where you are. It supports dual connection technology, allowing you to connect to upto two devices at a time. It provides a very comfortable fit and harmonious design too.

You can get this headphone for $150 on Amazon.

Anker Soundcore Life Q30 Wireless

For something more affordable, you should definitely check out the Anker Soundcore Life Q30 Wireless. Though they lose some unique features like haptic bass slider, they still deliver satisfying boom and thump. The default can also be optimised according to your liking with the help of graphic EQ and presets.

With this headphone you get Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) to help block out background noises that can disturb you. This can be useful when you are in places like office or any place where background noise can be a issue. This headphone also supports multi-device pairing, which allows you to connect it with upto two devices at a time. It can go upto 45 hours with the ANC on. It provides a very comfortable fit, which makes it suitable for long listening sessions.

You can get this headphone for $70.99 on Amazon.

Anker Soundcore Life Q20 Wireless

Anker Soundcore Life Q20 Wireless is a very affordable and good quality headphone. It has a heavy-bass sound profile, which means that it delivers intense thump and rumble by default. In addition to that, you can also increase or decrease the bass with the bass-effect button.

Its Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) suppresses ambient sounds like chatterings very well. This headphone can last for upto 31 hours of continuous playback with ANC on. You can use it wired, meaning that it can be used even when it is charging. It has a comfortable fit also.

You can get this headphone for $60.00 on Soundcore's official website or you can buy it for just $55 on Amazon.

Conclusion

This was all about the 6 best heaphones that have powerful bass. Now, it's upto you which headphone to choose. I recommend that you do more research before choosing any headphone. This can help you take out the pros and the cons to make a decision based on that.
