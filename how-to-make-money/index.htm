Make money online from the comfort of your home in 6 simple ways


Published on: MARCH 18, 2023

In today's world, anyone can make money online from the comfort of their home but the reason people aren't making any money online is that they don't know the right ways of doing it.

Today, let's know 6 simple and effective ways which you can use to make money online from your home.

How to make money from 6 simple ways

Below are the 6 ways which you can use to easily start making money online.

  1. Affiliate Marketing
  2. Sell products online
  3. Create and sell video content
  4. Become a digital content writer
  5. Create an app
  6. Develop websites

Affiliate Marketing

The first way to make money is Affiliate Marketing. Affiliate Marketing means to promote other companys' products in exchange of some commision that the company is willing to pay. The main advantage of using this method is that it requires very less money to get started.

To get started, you've to create a website or an account on any convenient platform like social media etc where you can easily promote the products of the company. There, you've to write description of the product that you want to sell. This way people can see and possibly buy the product using your link.

Now, you have to apply to the affiliate program of the company whose product you're promoting. Keep in mind that some companies don't have these kinds of programs. So if they don't have one, then you have to promote other companys' products and apply to their affiliate program (if they have one).

After getting into the affiliate program, you will provided custom links which you have to use in your post or website. You'll get commision only if a person buys the product through this custom link and not from any other link. After every 30 days or so you'll be paid based on the commision you have made.

Sell products online

Selling products online is also a good way to make money. You can sell products to multiple sales channels like Amazon, Ebay, Walmart etc or you can make your own shop and sell the products there. Selling products online can be done in two ways -

  • Doing an Ecommerce business - When doing an ecommerce business, you are both the manufacturer and the seller which means that you've to manufacture the products and then sell those to the customers. In ecommerce business the profit levels are high but the startup costs are also high.
  • Doing a dropshipping business - When doing a dropshipping business, you are only the seller which means that you sell the products from the manufacturer to the customers. In dropshipping business the startup costs are low but the profit levels are also low.

Both ways can be done by selling the products through your own shop or through multiple sales channels.

To make your own shop you can use Wordpress and then host it on the internet using Hostinger or you can just use Shopify if you want a easier process of making a shop. Then you've to list the products on your shop to start selling.

You can also list your products on multiple e-commerce businesses like Amazon, Walmart, E-bay etc. The benefit you get by listing the products on these businesses is that nearly everything is handled by the company whether it be returning an item, customer support or storage warehouses. But remember that these companies take good percentage of the total revenue.

This was all about how to list your products on the internet for sale. But now you've to promote to people and tell them about your products. This can be done through many ways like E-mail marketing, Creating your own blog post or running facebook or google ads. The content inside it should be something like coupon codes or free products on their first order. This way you will get more customers and eventually generate more revenue.

Create and sell video content

Creating and selling video content is another way of making money online. The way it works is that you sell the video content either on your own website or on some other platforms like Udemy, Skillshare etc. But you only get around 25%-75% of the total revenue when selling on these sites.

The main advantage of selling video content is that for each course that you make, you earn money every month or every year (based on your subscription model).

You've to make videos which are educational and creating these types of videos isn't that hard, you just need to pick a niche like business, programming, music etc and then develop contents on it. After making the videos you've to post it on the internet, this can be done by listing these videos either on your own website or on platforms like Udemy, Skillshare, Coursera.

You can also make entertaining videos. Just like educational videos, you've to pick niche like gaming, comedy etc and then make contents on it. You've to then post these contents on your own website or on platforms like Youtube, Twitch etc.

The next vital step is to promote these contents to people. This can be done through E-mail marketing, running Google or Facebook ads or by creating your own blog and giving your opinion on the contents that you've made. E-mails and ads should have content which is related to your videos and how it will help the user. Additionally, it should have a link which will take the user to where your videos are.

Become a digital content writer

If you have good writing skills and want to make money, then becoming a digital content writer is best for you. It is a profitable way which you can use to make an average $55,000-$65,000 a year. Apart from good writing skills, you should also have some basic SEO knowledge. Having some knowledge of basic HTML and CSS can also help you become a successful content writer.

You can start your career of digital content writer by doing freelance work on platforms like Fiverr, Freelancer or Upwork. You can also join content writing agencies like Express writer, Verblio etc to get more work opportunities. You can make a website to showcase your writing skills and work portfolios.

If you are new to content writing and want to develop your skills, then you can buy a course on Udemy to learn the necessary skillsets and knowledge to become a good content writing and start making money online.

Create an App

Creating an app is a great way to make money on the internet. You can create an app and sell it to a company or make and launch a free app to make money via in-app purchases and ads. An alternative would be to make a paid app with no in-app purchases and ads.

To make an app you should have some knowledge about app designing and how to make it interactive. If you don't know coding and are new to it, still you can make an app with the help of websites like Appy Pie, App Institute or Mobincube. These websites are really cheap and provide you with an intuitive app builder.

After you've done creating your app and have made sure that the app is working properly, you've to now upload it to App Store or Google Play Store or both. You've to wait until your app gets approved and thereafter, you've to now promote your app and earn money from it.

Develop websites

The average minimum salary of a freelance web developer is $85,000 per year. Moreover, the demand of web developers and digital designers is expected to grow by 20%-30% in the next 8 years. Because of this reason, now is the great time to get into this industry.

To get started, you've to pick an area within web development to specialize in. Mainly there are 3 areas that you can choose in web development -

  • Front-End developmer - As a Front-End developmer, your job is to make sure that the visual elements (which users can see and interact with) like buttons, animations etc are fully functional, user-friendly and pleasing. To become a Front-End developer you need to know HTML, CSS, Javascript and web designing.
  • Back-End developmer - As a Back-End developmer, your job is to improve the functionality and performance of the website by working the server-side of the website which includes writing code, managing the database and utilizing frameworks. You also have to make sure that the visual elements function correctly and efficiently. To become a Back-End developer, you need to know server-side languages like C++, Java, Ruby, Python and PHP.
  • Full-Stack development - As a Full-Stack developer, your job is to work as a Back-End developer and as a Front-End developer also. Which means that you have work on the front-end side of the website as well as the server-side of the website.

You've to choose the area which fits your passion and abilities. Doing this will help you save your precious time, energy and help you stand out among the competitors.

The next step is to learn how to code. Learning how to code is really very easy. You just have to take some help from courses and websites to get started. Examples of some websites can be Codecademy, Coursera and bit degree. These websites have courses that take you from zero to hero. You should really check these websites out if you want to learn how to code.

You are all set to start your web development career. Now, your next step should be to do something which can help you earn money like freelancing which is a very good way. To get started as a freelance web developer, you can go to sites like Fiverr, Freelancer or Upwork and offer your services. Slowly but surely you will earn money if you stay consistent. You can also promote your services in order to get more clients.

Conclusion

This was all about how to make money in 6 easy ways but now to scale it and earn even more, you've to constantly monitor and upgrade. You've to constantly upgrade your services whether it be adding a new product or making your site better. This will help you stand out among competitors

lastly, you've to also promote your products/services to more and more people. This will make sure that you get more traffic and eventually generate some more revenue.

That's it for now, I hope you learned something new!
